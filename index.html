Objective

Collect the objects in the maze in the correct order as quickly as possible.

How to play

Move forward by pressing 'W' or the up arrow key and backwards by pressing 'S' or down arrow key.

Turn left with 'A' and right with 'D' or the arrow keys.

To pickup objects press 'E' or the spacebar.

For touch screens: hold anywhere on the screen to activate the virtual joystick. Tap to pick up objects.

Select "Learn" to move around the maze and learn it's layout.

When you're ready select "Play" and try to get the fastest time!
